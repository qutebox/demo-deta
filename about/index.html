About Us

We help people who want to make the most of their day in life or work, to be the best they can be – being at the state of optimal physical and mental readiness when it matters most.

To this end, we developed a science-based AI digital performance coach that offers context-driven action plans aligned to each person’s health management and work performance goals – from daily optimization in fatigue management to sustainable peak performance; from preventive health to reversal of chronic illnesses. Our solution is highly personalized yet deployable at scale – to extend health span, save costs and achieve business KPIs.
